Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Overview

WHCSZZ Softworks ("we," "us," "our") operates whcs.site as a directory and safety index for third-party Minecraft clients. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit the site, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Depending on how the site is configured, we may collect:

  • Usage data — pages visited, referring site, browser type, device type, and approximate location (derived from IP address), typically gathered through analytics tools.
  • Cookies and local storage — small files used to remember preferences (such as theme settings) and to measure site traffic.
  • Voluntarily provided information — anything you send us directly, for example through our Discord server or a contact form, such as your username or message content.

We do not collect account passwords, payment card numbers, or government ID information. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of the third-party client websites this index links to — please review their own policies separately.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the site and the accuracy of the client index.
  • To understand aggregate traffic patterns (e.g. which listings are most viewed).
  • To respond to messages sent to us through Discord or other listed contact channels.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, spam, or security issues.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Analytics or hosting providers who process data on our behalf, under their own privacy and security terms.
  • Authorities, where required to comply with a legal obligation or valid legal request.

Third-Party Links

This site exists to index links to external, independently-operated Minecraft client websites (e.g. the "Website" buttons on each listing). Clicking through takes you off this site. We do not control, and are not responsible for, the content, security, or privacy practices of those external sites. Listing a client here does not constitute an endorsement of its safety or legitimacy — see our Terms of Service for more.

Cookies

If cookies or similar technologies are used, you can typically control or delete them through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

Data Retention

We retain usage data only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

Your Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To make a request, reach out through our Discord server.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
